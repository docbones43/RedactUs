What stays vs what's stripped

Stripped (identity)

  • Full name & next of kin
  • Date of birth
  • NHS / SSN / Insurance numbers
  • Home address & postcode / ZIP
  • Phone number & email
  • Hospital & clinician names
  • GP practice name
  • NI number / IBAN
  • Card details & transaction IDs
  • Policy & membership numbers
  • Hospital reference numbers

Kept (health data)

  • Cholesterol, LDL, HDL values
  • Blood glucose & HbA1c
  • Vitamin levels & hormones
  • Reference ranges & units
  • Symptoms & conditions
  • Medication names & doses
  • Your question to the AI
